WE ARE COHERE!

At COHERE, our mission is clear: to unleash the potential of refugees to determine their own futures on their own terms. We believe that everyone's future should be in their own hands, and we are dedicated to making this vision a reality. Imagine a world where everyone has control over their own future. At COHERE, we don’t just imagine it – we work every day to bring this vision to life. Our diverse and dedicated team collaborate and support refugee leaders with the tools and resources they need to build stronger, self-sustaining communities.

What we do

We partner with refugee-led organisations so they can transform their communities by offering critical services, skills training and opportunities. We do this through;

FUNDING

Create funding opportunities for RLOs and encourage donors to fund directly

CAPACITY

Training to ensure that RLOs can effectively respond to the needs of their communities

COORDINATION

Showcase the work of RLOs and connect them with other organisations

ADVOCACY

Influence donor practices, and support RLOs to tell their stories

Reframe

Fund Refugee leaders today

Reframe is a dynamic platform designed to amplify refugee voices and reshape narratives. From digital livelihoods and climate change to education and conflicts like the war in Sudan, Reframe connects refugee organisations, changemakers, fosters collaboration, and drives impactful social initiatives. Whether you're a storyteller, activist, or organisation, Reframe is your hub for creating positive change and inspiring action. Join us to reshape narratives and inspire action for a better world.
